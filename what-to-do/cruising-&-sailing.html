Journey through time on the Nile and explore the beauty of Egypt’s seas

Egypt is a country that has always honored the Nile. Add to that two glittering coastlines on the Red Sea and the Mediterranean, abundant sunshine, a temperate climate, and it’s no surprise that Egypt is an ideal destination for cruising and sailing. In Cairo, floating restaurants and traditional sailboats, or feluccas are a fun way to see the city lights or take in the sunset. Further south, there is no better way to explore the sites of ancient Egypt than onboard a Nile cruise. And no visit to the Red Sea would be complete without a day spent on a boat to dive, snorkel, fish, or simply relax and take in the sunshine on deck. River or sea, there’s plenty to do offshore in Egypt!
Nile Cruises
Discover the beauty of the Nile and explore ancient Egypt while floating down the river onboard a classic Nile cruise.
Dahabeyas

Want to cruise the Nile but prefer a more intimate, luxurious, and exclusive experience? You

can charter a private Dahabeya to sail between Luxor and Aswan, and stop at your leisure

at places off the beaten path.

Yachting & Sailing

Head out for adventure-filled days on the water, exploring Egypt’s seas and shorelines from a

sailboat or yacht! On the Red Sea, boat trips are a highlight not to be missed.
